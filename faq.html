What is HRV?

HRV is the phenomenon of variation in time interval between your heart beats. It's called heart rate variability (HRV). You can find more explaination and also further reading here.

Which devices are supported?

At the moment Microsoft Band 2 and some ANT+ compatible devices. You can find a list of possible compatible devices on this site. Besides that the following devices were actually tested with HRV Band:

  • Garmin Forerunner 735XT
  • vivosmart HR
  • Garmin HRM-Tri (TM)

I have Android version 4.4 or higher and can't connect my device

If you use Microsoft Band 2 you need to install the Microsoft Band app on your device and connect your MS Band with it. If you use an ANT+ device your phone needs to be compatible with ANT+. You can check if your phone is compatible with ANT+ on this site. If your phone is not on the list or the ANT+ drivers are not installed on your phone yet you can install them manually. Just Download the following apps in the Google Play Store:

  • ANT Radio Service
  • ANT+ Plugins Service
  • ANT USB Service
To test your installation you can use the ANT+ Plugin Sampler.

Is it indifferent what device I use?

The quality of the measured heart beats differ from one device to another. You can achieve best results by using a chest belt. If you use a device on your wrist check if the sensors are on the inside of your wrist. This improves also improves your measurement.
