Hi there!
We developed a first prototype of HRV Band on a hackathon in June 2016. Now we are releasing a full functional app to measure your heart rate variability with Microsoft Band 2 and compatible ANT+ devices. We also do not advertise our app or get any money from your data as told in our privacy policy down below. We develop in our free time and hoping you enjoy our app. If you have any kind of feedback don't hesitate and contact us: hrvband+feedback@gmail.com

Privacy

HRV Band does save all your data on your smart phone. Nothing is sent to any server! If you are looking for a formal Privacy Policy click here: Privacy Policy
