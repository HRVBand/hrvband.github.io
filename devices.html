Which devices are supported?

At the moment Microsoft Band 2 and some ANT+ compatible devices are supported in HRV Band. HRV Band was tested with the following measurement devices:

  • Garmin Forerunner 735XT
  • vivosmart HR
  • Garmin HRM-Tri (TM)
Besides that you can find a list of possible compatible devices on this site. If you use HRV Band successfully with a device not listed above contact us to help us update the list :)

Compatible Phones

First of all you need a phone with Android 4.4 or higher. Besides HRV Band you need some other applications!

Microsoft Band 2

If you use Microsoft Band 2 you need to install the Microsoft Band app on your device and connect your MS Band with it.

ANT+

If you use an ANT+ device your phone needs to be compatible with ANT+. You can check if your phone is compatible with ANT+ on this site. If your phone is not on the list or the ANT+ drivers are not installed on your phone yet you can install them manually. Just Download the following apps from the Google Play Store:

  • ANT Radio Service
  • ANT+ Plugins Service
  • ANT USB Service
To test your installation you can use the ANT+ Plugin Sampler.
